Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

Engraft collects no personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What data we collect

None. Engraft stores your scripture verses and review progress exclusively in local storage on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-party services

Engraft uses RevenueCat solely to process in-app subscription purchases (Engraft Pro). RevenueCat receives an anonymous device identifier and purchase receipt to validate your subscription. No name, email, or personally identifiable information is shared. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Engraft does not use any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.

Device permissions

Engraft does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device capability. The app functions entirely offline.

Children's privacy

Because Engraft collects no data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Data deletion

All app data can be removed at any time by deleting the app from your device. No data exists anywhere else.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change our data practices, this page will be updated with a new effective date. We will not introduce data collection without updating this policy first.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@engraftapp.com.
